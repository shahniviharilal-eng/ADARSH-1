The Story
How We Started

ANI ONE IN INDIA began in 2026 as a small side project — a personal media player that a group of developers built to manage their local anime collection. As word spread through the community, it grew into the platform you see today.

We noticed that most anime platforms were either cluttered with ads, locked behind expensive subscriptions, or simply didn't work well on mobile. We wanted something different: a platform that felt as beautiful as the anime it served.

Today ANI ONE IN INDIA i hope is used by thousands of fans globally. We're still a small, passionate team — and we intend to stay that way, so we can keep making decisions that put fans first.

Short version: ANI ONE IN INDIA stores your uploaded anime data locally in your browser. We don't collect personal information, sell your data, or run ads. This policy explains the full details.

1. Information We Collect

ANI ONE IN INDIA is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information necessary to provide the service:

  • Uploaded Content: Video files, images, and metadata (title, genre, description) you upload are stored locally in your browser's localStorage. They never leave your device.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous, aggregated analytics data such as page views and feature usage to improve the platform. This data cannot identify you personally.
  • Contact Form Data: If you contact us via our contact form, we collect your name, email address, and message content to respond to your inquiry.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the ANI ONE IN INDIA platform and its features
  • To respond to your support requests and inquiries
  • To improve user experience based on anonymous usage patterns
  • To detect and prevent technical issues and abuse
  • To comply with legal obligations where required

We never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage & Security

All anime uploads, watch history, and preferences are stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage. This means:

  • Your content stays on your device and is never transmitted to our servers
  • Clearing your browser data will remove your ANI ONE IN INDIA library
  • Other devices or browsers cannot access your library unless you export and import it

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect any data that does pass through our servers (e.g., contact form submissions), including encrypted HTTPS connections and secure server infrastructure.

4. Cookies & Local Storage

ANI ONE IN INDIA uses browser localStorage (not traditional cookies) to store your anime library and app preferences. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies.

You can clear your browser's localStorage at any time through your browser settings. Note that this will delete your ANI ONE IN INDIA library data.

5. Third-Party Services

ANI ONE IN INDIA may use the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google Fonts: We load web fonts from Google's servers. Google may collect font request data. See Google Privacy Policy.
  • jsDelivr CDN: We load icon libraries from jsDelivr. See their privacy policy at jsdelivr.com.

We do not integrate any advertising networks, social media trackers, or analytics platforms that collect personally identifiable information.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: You can request a copy of any personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Deletion: You can request that we delete your personal data from our systems.
  • Right to Correction: You can request that we correct inaccurate personal data.
  • Right to Portability: You can request your data in a portable format.
  • Right to Object: You can object to our processing of your personal data.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@anioneinindia.io.

7. Children's Privacy

ANI ONE IN INDIA is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately so we can delete it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting a prominent notice on our website and updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Your continued use of ANI ONE IN INDIA after changes become effective constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
